Virtual Reality, the technology of the future

Virtual reality and augmented reality are two technologies that have been appearing in many electronic devices. Despite their similar names, they have different characteristics and objectives: while the first takes you to a new environment created by computer, the second includes projections of content and complementary information in the real world.

Both need an intermediary to be accessed, which can be an application or an accessory, but offer totally different experiences for users.
